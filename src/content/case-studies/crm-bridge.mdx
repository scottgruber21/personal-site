## The Bottleneck
At RCCS, our marketing-to-sales handoff was broken. We were using clunky, dated templates that sent leads into a black hole. Our admin staff had to manually re-type every single form submission into Microsoft Dynamics CRM. It was slow and it meant our sales team was getting leads days too late.

## The Hack
I didn't want a "no-code" band-aid that would break the first time WordPress updated. I needed to build a real data pipeline.

### The API Handshake
I used Zapier to connect the dots, but I avoided the standard "out of the box" settings. Instead, I wrote custom code inside Zapier to handle the handshake between the Gravity Forms API and the Dynamics API. This let us:
* **Clean the data** before it ever hit the CRM database.
* **Route leads instantly** to the right rep based on the specific services the prospect selected.

### Performance First
I ditched the bloated website templates and hard-coded the landing pages from scratch. I wanted a lean, fast path to the "submit" button.

## The Result
Once the pipeline was live, the friction vanished:
* **Volume:** Lead capture jumped by 55% because the pages worked on mobile.
* **Speed:** We cut the administrative response time by 40%.
* **Trust:** Sales finally had accurate data waiting for them the second a lead hit the site.
